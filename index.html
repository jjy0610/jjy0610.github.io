About Me

I am currently pursuing an M.Eng. in Financial Engineering at the School of Operations Research and Information Engineering, Cornell University. I hold a bachelor’s degree in Economics with a minor in Philosophy from National Taiwan University, and I also completed an exchange program at Peking University.

My research and professional interests lie in the application of data science in the financial industry, with a particular focus on natural language processing. I am always open to exploring new technologies that are challenging yet potentially transformative for the financial sector.
